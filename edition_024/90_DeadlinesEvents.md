# Events

### ALICE Workshop:

The [Artificial Life, Intelligence, Complexity and Evolution (ALICE) workshop](https://aliceworkshop.org/) happened between February 2nd and 6th, at the IT University of Copenhagen. You can visit their webpage to see details about the topics, pictures and discussions. Organizer Elias Najarro tells us that they are getting ready to upload podcasts and sessions from the workshop to the [ALICE Youtube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@ALICE_Workshop), so, as they say, be sure to Like and Subscribe!
