# A Word from the Team

Welcome to the 25th issue of the ALife Newsletter!

In this edition, we explore several ALife sims. We interview the creator of the evolutionary sim Soup of Life, review a paper about a multicellular reproduction sim, and share an asymmetric particle sim called Particle Life in 3D.

This month, we also highlight the connections between artificial and biological life as we contemplate transferring virtual worm brains into virtual flies and draw parallels between researching cognition using both synthetic biology and actual bacteria like *E. coli*.
