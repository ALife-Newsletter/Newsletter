# Origins of life: the possible and the actual

This theme issue of Philosophical Transactions B ([Origins of life: the possible and the actual](https://royalsocietypublishing.org/rstb/issue/380/1936)) should interest any reader who likes Origins of Life research, as well as related topics: Agency, Transitions, Evolution, Protocells, and of course Artificial Life!
The issue is from last year, but should remain relevant for many years to come.
