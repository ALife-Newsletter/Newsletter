# Particle Life in 3D

Elijas Najarro [shared](https://bsky.app/profile/najarro.science/post/3miej7nquvc2n) a mesmerizing implementation of an [asymmetric particle simulation in 3D](https://najarro.science/pl/) that runs on browsers.
Go have a look, toggle the 3D setting and play with the parameters!
