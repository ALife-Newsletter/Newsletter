Animats

By Lana

The first ALife project I ever worked on, during a 1-month internship in what would later become my PhD lab, was about modeling rats neurons to reproduce the dynamics of an animat. Animats are robots that are connected to real, living neurons - often rat neurons. As a research topic, animats are not as popular as they used to, but to me they remain the quintessential cyborgs.
