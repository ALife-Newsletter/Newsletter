Remember when text generators were tiny neural nets that you'd train on your own little datasets to see what happens? I do! That's probably when I had the most fun with anything AI related, and so it reminds me of simpler times... At the time, I trained one on my Twitter archive and gave it its own Twitter account. A bumbling, incoherent distillation of my Twitter personality. I also trained one on the New York Library's open archive of cruise menus and made it generate unholy dishes... which is probably why I loved this post by Janelle Shane:
